Maya如何获取拍屏面板参数？¶

optionVar命令可以获取一些Maya选项设置的参数

import maya.cmds as cmds

for elem in cmds.optionVar(l=True):

    if "playblast" in elem.lower():
        print(elem)
        print(cmds.optionVar(q=elem))
